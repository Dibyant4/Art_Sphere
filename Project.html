ArtSphere

An online art showcase platform.

ArtSphere is a concept project I designed and developed to create a space where artists, designers, and creatives can showcase their work and connect with an audience. The goal was to design a clean, modern, and visually engaging website that balances aesthetics with usability. A visually clean and professional portfolio-style website that highlights art first. Fully responsive design that works smoothly across devices. A strong brand identity for ArtSphere that feels both modern and approachable. Improved usability through consistent navigation and interaction patterns.
